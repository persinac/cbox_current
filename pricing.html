Subscription Plan

We offer a single, flexible monthly plan to accommodate your dynamic community. Based on the number of members per month, and free for the first 10 members, you will get access to all of the features presented.

- $2.00, one-time activation fee (this gets your box set up and ready for members)

- $1.50/member, capped at $200.00

- Access to all features (stat tracking, regional comparison, box metrics*)

- Future updates and upgrades at no cost and 24/7 support

- First month is on us!

* Box metrics are available only to Administrators of the box. Whether they choose to display to the box is entirely up to them.
